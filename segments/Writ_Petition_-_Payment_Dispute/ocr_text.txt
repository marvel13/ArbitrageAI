ORDER
The captioned petition is filed by the petitioner
seeking the following reliefs:
"1. Issue a Writ of Mandamus or any other
appropriate Writ, order or direction, directing the
Respondents to release the amounts as against the
final bill submitted on 22.02.2023 and 06.09.2023
(Annexure B) as per the agreed terms specified in
Clause 9 of the Contract/work order.
2. And pass any such other order that this Court
deems fit in the facts and circumstances of the case in
the interest of justice and equity.
2.
The petitioner is compelled to knock the
doors of this Court feeling aggrieved by the inaction on
the part of the respondents in not considering the
representation submitted by the petitioner.
3. The grievance of the petitioner is that it was
allotted the work order for construction of complex. The
petitioner claims that it has completed the project
assigned to it and respondents inspite of completion of
