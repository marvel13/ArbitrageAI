1. As you are aware, the work of Construction of Extension to Assembly
Integration & Test facility (AITF-1) at ISITE campus was completed
on 15/05/2022 with a total delay of 483 days with respect to its
scheduled completion period. With regard to the delays, following
may be noted:
a. For every Major Work, URSC records the hindrances
systematically;
b. The hindrances, reasons for the hindrance and its impact on the
progress of work are analysed by the appropriate authority in
URSC & Department of Space (DOS) to make the
recommendations for grant of final Extension of Time (EOT)
required for the Work & for Final bill settlement;
c. The final approving authority for EOT is the concerned Tender
Approving Authority. In case of the extant work, the same is
Secretary, DOS with the concurrence of Member (Finance), Space
Commission.
d. With regard to the subject work also, the due process was
scrupulously followed by URSC/ISRO whereby all the hindrances
which were encountered during the progress of work and which
were claimed by you vide your letter no.URC/ISRO AITF-1
Extn/2022/1057 dated 09/09/2022 were analysed in detail to
ascertain its eligibility for extension of time as per clause 5.2 of
agreement conditions including those hindrances which had
occurred during the period 09/03/2022 to 15/05/2022.
e. During the above scrutiny, URSC has calculated the hindrance
periods with due consideration on overlapping of hindrances and
determined the justifiable hindrances and unjustifiable hindrances.
f. Hence, you may note that the final call on Extension of Time for the
work and the extent of compensation to be levied & the extent of

escalation payment was made up to 08/03/2022.

5.2 With respect to your claim no.2 (Releasing of illegal/arbitrary levy
amount), it may be noted that as stated in Para 4 & 5.1 above, the
compensation for delay was decided by Department with due
consideration on Clause 5 of agreement conditions and the grant of

letter no. C&MG/ISITE/EXTN AITF-1/2024-25/EIC(C)/70 dated
20/05/2024. Hence, the compensation for delay levied is strictly as
per contract conditions.
5.3 With respect to your claim no.3 (Compensation for the expenses
incurred during COVID-19 stay period), it is to note that the work was
temporarily suspended due to outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic. As
per clause 15 of agreement conditions, for suspension of work by
Engineer In-charge, the Contractor will be allowed an extension of
time for completion, however, no other claims in this respect for
compensation or otherwise will be admissible as the extraneous
event of Covid-19 pandemic is beyond the control of both the
contracting parties, which includes URSC. As stated in Sl. No. 3
above, "An FM clause in the contract frees both parties from
contractual liability or obligation when prevented by such events from
fulfilling their obligations under the contract." Accordingly, the
extension of time for COVID-19 pandemic period was provided
without cost to both URC & URSC. Hence, your claim for
compensation of expenses incurred during COVID-19 stay period is
not admissible. As per clause 5.2 & 5.4 of agreement conditions, fair
and reasonable extension of time for force majeure period has been
provided.
5.4 With respect to your claim no.4 (Compensation for the prolongation
period expenditures), there is no such provision for payment of
prolongation charges in the agreement conditions.
5.5 With respect to your claim no.5 (Additional Interest burden due to
withholding of 13.20 Crores at the rate of 18% interest per annum),
it may please be noted that, as per point no.17 of General guidelines
